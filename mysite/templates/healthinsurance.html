Health Insurance

Benefits of Health Insurance

Life is a surprise package of happiness and challenges. Sudden illnesses, accidental injuries and medical emergencies can throw us off guard anytime. While they drain us emotionally, the hospitalization and medicinal expenses can also burn a big hole in your pocket, owing to the soaring prices of medical services.

Financial Stability

A health insurance plan provides you with financial stability during medical emergencies which may involve huge healthcare costs. A health insurance plan offers coverage for expenses incurred during in-patient hospitalization along with that incurred pre & post-hospitalization up to the promised sum insured.

Peace of Mind

A health insurance plan helps you maintain peace of mind by taking care of all financial needs related to the medication.

Timely and Good Quality Medical Treatment

A health insurance plan helps you in getting timely and good quality medical treatment. Insurance companies have tie-ups with hospitals where you can get medical treatment in a cashless manner without any paperwork or any other hassle.

Tax Benefits

The premium paid for a health insurance plan qualifies for a tax deduction of up to Rs. 25,000 and Rs. 50,000 for individuals below 60 years of age and individuals above 60 years respectively under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act.
